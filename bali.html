Overview

Discover the rich history, culture, and breathtaking attractions of this destination. Explore centuries-old landmarks, museums, and experience vibrant local traditions that make this destination a must-see.

Things to Do

Take a guided city tour and visit historic landmarks.
Relax on the pristine beaches or go for a swim.
Enjoy adventurous activities like hiking, snorkeling, and zip-lining.
Explore local markets and savor authentic cuisine.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is from March to May when the weather is mild, and there are many cultural festivals to experience. Avoid the rainy season from September to November for a more comfortable trip.

Travel Tips

Carry local currency, as some small vendors may not accept credit cards.
Respect local customs and dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
Stay hydrated and apply sunscreen when exploring outdoor attractions.
Learn a few basic phrases in the local language to enhance your experience.
